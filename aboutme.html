ME

Hello, I'm Nur Aliya Syazwani Binti Hishammudin, a 20-year-old, currently residing in Pahang. I was born on 15th June 2004 at Hospital Kota Bharu in Kelantan. Growing up amidst the picturesque landscapes of Pahang has shaped my perspective and instilled in me a deep appreciation for nature and community. I am currently pursuing my studies at UiTM Kedah, where I am deeply engaged in exploring library management. This educational journey not only enriches my understanding but also fuels my passion to contribute positively to society. I am committed to leveraging my education to make a meaningful impact in my community and beyond. Throughout my academic journey, I have taken various examinations, includes:

  • UPSR (Ujian Peperiksaan Sekolah Rendah): 1A 4B 1C

  • PT3 (Pentaksiran Tingkatan 3): 4A 4B 1C 1E

  • SMA (Sijil Menengah Agama): Jayyid

  • SPM (Sijil Pelajaran Malaysia): 3A 3B 3C

  • STAM (Sijil Tinggi Agama Malaysia): Jayyid

EDUCATION

PRIMARY SCHOOL

I completed my primary education at Sekolah Kebangsaan Jengka 14, where I laid the foundation for my academic journey. One of the significant examinations during this period was the UPSR (Ujian Pencapaian Sekolah Rendah). I am pleased to have achieved 1A 4B 1C in the UPSR examination, reflecting my commitment to learning and academic success during my formative years.

SECONDARY SCHOOL

I attended Sekolah Menengah Agama Al-Khairiah in Temerloh, Pahang, where I completed my secondary education. During this period, I undertook several significant examinations, including PT3 (Pentaksiran Tingkatan 3), where I achieved 4A, 4B, 1C, and 1E. Additionally, I obtained the SMA (Sijil Menengah Agama) with a 'Jayyid' rating, reflecting my proficiency in religious studies. In my SPM (Sijil Pelajaran Malaysia) examinations, I secured 3A, 3B, and 3C grades. These achievements highlight my consistent dedication to academic excellence and holistic learning throughout my secondary school years.

STAM ERA

After completing my secondary education at Sekolah Menengah Agama Al-Khairiah in Temerloh, Pahang, I continued my studies at Kulliah Al-Lughah Waddin As-Sultan Abu Bakar in Pekan, Pahang. One of the significant examinations I undertook during this period was STAM (Sijil Tinggi Agama Malaysia), where I achieved a 'Jayyid' rating. This accomplishment reflects my dedication to deepening my knowledge and proficiency. My educational journey at Kulliah Al-Lughah Waddin As-Sultan Abu Bakar has equipped me with valuable insights, skills and also experiences.

DEGREE

After successfully completing STAM (Sijil Tinggi Agama Malaysia), I enrolled at Universiti Teknologi Mara (UiTM) in Sungai Petani, Kedah, where I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Information Science (Honours) in Library Management. I am currently in Semester 2 of my studies. This program has provided me with a comprehensive understanding of library sciences and information management, allowing me to meet nice people and expand my network. It has also been a journey of acquiring new knowledge and gaining valuable experiences, preparing me for a career dedicated to organizing and preserving knowledge. I am eager to continue exploring this field and contributing meaningfully to the world of information and education.
